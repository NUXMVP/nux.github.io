Use 1: Quest Reward Programs

NUX IDs can be seamlessly integrated into quest reward programs, offering a secure system for tracking and distributing rewards. Both onchain activity and submitted data using NUX IDs can be analyzed for rewards. Users can complete and report on specific NUX quests to notify the system of completion. The NUX system, designed to operate at the speed of trust, will verify activities as needed, extending trust to NUX IDs.
